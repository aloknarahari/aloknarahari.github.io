—About Me—
Resume

I started off as an econ major who became fascinated with policy, regulation, enforcement, and market failures. I therefore decided to try my hand at law school to get an inside look at our system's underlying framework.

I've worked at a variety of organizations including the SFDA's office, a legal startup, the Berkeley City Council, and the California Chamber of Commerce. I've also conducted research on economic clusters with a research group to create well-paying jobs in the energy sector.

In college I worked as a news reporter for the Daily Californian where I experienced the hectic yet rewarding world of journalism. I also co-founded a branch of 180 Degrees Consulting, an international pro-bono consultancy, to help out nonprofit institutions in the Bay.
